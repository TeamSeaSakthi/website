About Team Sea Sakthi

Team Sea Sakthi made history as the inaugural and sole Indian team at the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge in 2022. Comprising a dedicated team of 13 students, they crafted India's pioneering energy boat, 'Yali', marking a significant milestone. Building on their success, they unveiled 'Yali 2.0' in the subsequent edition, showcasing their commitment to innovation. Their exceptional journey continues as they proudly showcase India's ingenuity for the third consecutive time on the international platform, reaffirming their status as pioneers in sustainable marine technology.

About

Welcome to the Garage at Kumaraguru College, a dynamic hub where automotive enthusiasts from various domains come together to innovate and excel. This vibrant space hosts over 400 undergraduate students collaborating across 13 specialized teams, including Formula, Go-Kart, and sustainable initiatives like E-Bike. Supported by 20 industry partners, these teams work tirelessly on pioneering projects, aiming to shape the future of automotive engineering. Our community boasts over 100 titles and a network of 1400+ alumni, reflecting our commitment to excellence and innovation. The Garage is not just a workspace; it's a launchpad for aspiring engineers to transform their ideas into reality.

Yali 1.0

Team Sea Sakthi proudly represents India as the first and only team to participate in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge. Comprising a dedicated 13-member crew, this innovative team has engineered a remarkable vessel: Yali 1.0, the nation’s first energy boat.

Yali 1.0 is a testament to cutting-edge technology and sustainable design, featuring:

Aluminium Fabricated Cockpit 400W Solar Panel as Secondary Power Source 9.6 kWh Lithium-Ion Battery as Primary Power Source Powered by a 6kW Propulsion System Their exceptional performance earned them an impressive 6th place overall globally, and they were also honored with the Communication Prize for their outstanding efforts in sharing their vision and innovations with the world.

Yali 2.0

Team Sea Sakthi continues to lead the way once again in sustainable maritime innovation as the first and only team from India to participate in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge. With a dedicated 10-member crew in their second year, they have now introduced YALI 2.0, the nation’s first hydrogen-powered boat.
YALI 2.0 exemplifies cutting-edge technology and sustainable design, featuring:

Hydrogen Fuel Cell Powered Energy Boat Carbon Fibre Monocoque Structure Custom-Designed Electric Propulsion System

Their exceptional performance earned them the 6th overall position globally and the Communication Prize for two consecutive years. Additionally, they received a special recognition award, the Monaco Townhall Cup, in their second year.

Yali 3.0

Team Sea Sakthi: Pioneers in Sustainable Maritime Innovation For the third consecutive year, we at Team Sea Sakthi are proud to represent India as the only team qualified to participate in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge (MEBC). With greater determination than ever, our 13-member crew has developed YALI 3.0, showcasing the pinnacle of sustainable boating technology.
YALI 3.0 features:

Twin Propulsion System: Engineered for high performance, capable of reaching speeds up to 20 knots. Indigenous LFP Battery Pack: Designed for high performance under heavy current loads. Monocoque Structure: Composed of pineapple fiber, offering sustainable strength, high performance, and eco-chic appeal. Building on our previous successes—ranking 6th overall globally, winning the Communication Prize for two consecutive years, and receiving the Monaco Townhall Cup, we are setting new benchmarks in clean energy boating. As we prepare for MEBC 2024, we aim for the podium, bringing our innovative spirit and commitment to sustainability to the global stage once again.

Who Are We?

Team Sea Sakthi is excited to participate in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge 2024 as the official team representing our institution. Our team comprises of 10 passionate students who are dedicated to developing a unique hybrid boat concept.

Mission

To induce a revolution of electric mobility in the maritime sector by incorporating concepts of cleaner technologies.

Vision

YALI 4.0

Elevating marine innovation with a bespoke 15 kW axial flux motor, delivering precise, high-efficiency propulsion. Its advanced Balsa-Flax composite cockpit merges lightweight strength with sustainable resilience. Cutting-edge AR Glasses provide pilots with comprehensive 360° awareness and real-time insights, while an IP67-rated PDU featuring HVIL and IRM technology streamlines power management, reduces wiring complexity by 60%, and enhances safety—defining a new pinnacle of intelligent, eco-conscious marine performance.
